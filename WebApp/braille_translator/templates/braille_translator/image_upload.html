About Braille Image OCR

This feature allows you to upload images containing braille text and extract the content using optical character recognition (OCR).

  • Upload clear images of braille text
  • Automatic image preprocessing and enhancement
  • Braille dot detection and pattern recognition
  • Translation from braille to regular text
Tips for best results:
  • Use high-resolution images (300 DPI or higher)
  • Ensure good lighting and minimal shadows
  • Keep the camera parallel to the braille surface
  • Avoid reflections and glare
